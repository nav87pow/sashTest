Ending economic disparities in weaker communities

Trustlines App has a bold mision: to allow anyone, anywhere (with a mobile phone and internet) to create and trade in their own currencies, independant of the current financial realities they face.

Using blockchain technology, this reference implementation app’s end-users are your grandma or your local community leader. That meant simplifying incredibly complex concepts while funneling users to use the app a payments app.
